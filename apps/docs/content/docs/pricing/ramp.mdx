## Off-ramp (transfer NGN out, receive cNGN)

**Platform fee:**
You are charged 0.1%(0.001) capped at 200 NGN

**Base withdrawal fee:**
For transaction below **50,000** NGN is 55 NGN

## On-ramp (transfer cNGN out, receive NGN)

**Platform fee:**
You are charged 0.001% capped at 200 NGN

**Base deposit fee:**
For transaction below _50,000_ NGN is 55 NGN

Stamp duty: **50 NGN**<br/>
Blockchain/gas fee: **(Network dependent, but usually less than $0.1)

### NOTE:

- There is a ₦50 Statutory stamp duty charge for deposits above ₦10,000, and the Central Bank of Nigeria collects this.
- Blockchain fees fluctuate and depend on network congestion.

## TRANSACTION RANGE

### Fiat single transaction limit:

100.00 NGN > 1,000,000 NGN

### cNGN single transaction limit:

100.00 cNGN > ∞ cNGN

Note: if you require a higher single transaction limit, please message the team or email admin@flintapi.io

### OTC Level Transaction:

If you require personal service to handle single large transactions for your business or HNI customers, do well to message the team or email ola@flintapi.io
