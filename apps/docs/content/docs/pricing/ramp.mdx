## OFF-RAMP (Transfer NGN out, receive cNGN)

**Platform fee:**
You are charged **0.1%(0.001)** capped at 200 NGN + **7.5% VAT**

**Base withdrawal fee:**
For transaction below **50,000** NGN is 55 NGN + **7.5% VAT**

**VALUE ADDED TAX(VAT)** <br/>
**7.5%** of “_fee_”
- Base VAT: 7.5% of 55 NGN = 4.125 NGN
- Platform fee VAT: 7.5% of 0.001<sub>capped</sub>

## ON-RAMP (Transfer cNGN out, receive NGN)

**Platform fee:**
You are charged **0.1%(0.001)** capped at **200 NGN** + **Stamp Duty**<sub>if applies</sub>

**Base deposit fee:**
For transaction below _50,000_ NGN is **55 NGN** + **Stamp Duty**<sub>if applies</sub>

**Stamp duty:**
50 NGN (for transactions above 10,000 NGN)

**Gas/Blockchain network fee:** 
(Network dependent, but usually less than $0.1)

### NOTE:
- VAT: This is **7.5% of the FEE**, and the government collects this.
- STAMP DUTY: There is a **₦50 Statutory stamp duty** charge for transfers above ₦10,000, and the Central Bank of Nigeria collects this.
- GAS FEE: Blockchain fees fluctuate and depend on network congestion.
